Samwise Gamgee

Samwise Gamgee is from the "Lord of the Rings" trilogy written by JRR Tolkien. He lives in the world of Middle Earth and his hometown is the Shire. He is a hobbit and is tasked with the duty of protecting Frodo Baggins as he makes his trek towards Mordor in order to destroy the one ring. Here are some reasons why I admire this character

  • Samwise Gamgee has the best mental fortitude
  • Without him, Frodo would've died ten thousand times
